What is Boßeln ?

Boßeln is a traditional German sport that involves throwing a small ball (the "boßel") along a predetermined course, typically through rural landscapes or along roadsides. Players use a special stick to propel the ball as far as possible while adhering to the rules of the game. It's often played in teams, with each team taking turns to throw the ball and aiming to cover the course in the fewest throws. Boßeln is popular during the winter months and is enjoyed as both a competitive sport and a social activity.
